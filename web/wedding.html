Accommodation. Why not stay on the estate and stumble to your room just a short walk from the reception.

South Devon's population balloons in the summer months, we suggest you make a booking as early as possible to make sure you're not disappointed.

We recommend that you stay at Dartington in their hotel. If you do wish to stay at Dartington please let the hotel know that you are guests of our wedding so that you get the reduced wedding rate for your stay.

ACCOMMODATION SUGGESTIONS:

  • DARTINGTON HALL

    Dartington Courtyard Rooms offer B&B accommodation in an informal and unique setting on a 1200-acre estate amongst medieval buildings.

  • THE COTT INN

    One of the Oldest and Best thatched inns in Britain, featuring 5 Boutique Rooms & Fine Dining, welcoming guests since 1320AD.

  • THE OLD FORGE

    The Old Forge is a 600-year old stone building offering accommodation in Totnes, 450 yards from the town centre and 0.6 miles from Totnes Castle.

  • ROYAL SEVEN STARS

    The Royal Seven Stars Hotel is located in the vibrant town of Totnes just a stone’s throw from the River Dart and perfectly positioned to explore all that South Devon has to offer.

  • THE BULL INN

    Featuring a restaurant and a bar, The Bull Inn is located in Totnes, 300 metres from Totnes Castle. Guests at the hotel can enjoy a cooked breakfast.
