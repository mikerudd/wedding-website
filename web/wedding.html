DIRECTIONS

From experience, Sat Navs will get you to where you need to go. However, in Devon the way they take you might mean navigating some tiny grass covered lanes and squeezing yourself past a tractor or two. To help you find your way, we'll put some arrows out along the way.

Shilstone

Modbury

Devon

PL21 0TW

  • Follow the A381 towards Kingsbridge for a couple of miles.
  • You'll come across a junction, signposted Plymouth/Modbury(A379) - take a left. DO NOT continue onto Kingsbridge.
  • Follow the road for a couple of miles until you come to a roundabout. Go straight over (2nd exit, signposted Modbury.
  • Continue along this road, you'll go down a steep hill and across a bridge before coming to another roundabout, again straight over (2nd exit, signposted Modbury).
  • Continue along this road, for about 10 minutes (it gets windy) until you get to Modbury.
  • When you get to Modbury you'll take a sharp left turn in front on the village Co-Op.
  • 50m past here BEFORE the zebra crossing, you'll need to turn RIGHT! Onto Brownston St.
  • Follow this road for a few minutes. You'll want to take the 3rd left you come across.
  • Follow this lane for a few minutes, you'll go down a hill over a narrow bridge and past Week farm. Eventually you'll come to junction, take a RIGHT!
  • In a few seconds you'll have arrived @ Shilstone (on your right).
